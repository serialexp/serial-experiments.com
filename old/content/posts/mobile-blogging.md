Since I'm generally unable to sit down at my computer and publish a blog post, I wanted to try and see if maybe I could make it work from my mobile phone.

Working Copy (a IOS app) allows me to check out my blog repo remote, and edit the files in it, but while it works, it isn't exactly buildt to do markdown editing.

That said, all the other apps that allow editing either cost a ton of money, or don’t open files outside their own walled garden, so I think I’ll keep it here.

I only wish there was a way to get it to recognize the special hugo opening tags at the top of the file.
